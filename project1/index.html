About Us

Welcome to DEHA Games

Crafting Worlds. Forging Legends.

At DEHA Games, we are a small, independent team with a big passion for Souls games. Born from late-night brainstorming sessions, endless cups of coffee, and a shared love for Souls games, our studio is dedicated to building rich, immersive worlds where every boss is stronger than the next and every adventure feels personal.

We believe that great RPGs dont need to be like Elden Ring or Bloodborne massive budgets—they can be just as unique and enjoyable(even if it is 2D). Thats why we focus on creating thoughtful, handcrafted experiences where players can lose themselves in the narrative, tackle bosses so hard it makes you question your intelligence, and explore dynamic, living environments shaped by their actions. Whether its a retro-inspired pixel RPG, a turn-based tactical journey, or a deep lore-driven fantasy epic, we aim to make every project feel like an unforgettable journey.

What sets us apart is our dedication to detail. From intricate lore and meaningful quests to original soundtracks and lovingly animated sprites, we pour our creativity into every corner of our games. We're not chasing trends—we're telling stories we care about and inviting players to be part of them.

We are currently hard at work on expanding our team, and we cant wait to share more with you. In the meantime, check out our Team page and get to know our seniors, or drop us a message—our email is at the bottom of the page.

Thanks for stopping by. The campfires warm, the maps are drawn, and the journey is just beginning.

Lets forge something epic.
