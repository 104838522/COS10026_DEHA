Welcome to DEHA Games

Crafting Worlds. Forging Legends.

At DEHA Games, we are a small, independent team with a big passion for Souls games. Born from a common shared love for Souls games, our team is dedicated to building rich, immersive worlds where every boss is stronger than the next and every adventure feels personal.

We believe that great RPGs dont need to be like Elden Ring or Bloodborne massive budgets—they can be just as unique and enjoyable(even if it is 2D). Thats why we focus on creating thoughtful, experiences where players can lose themselves in the narrative, tackle bosses so hard it makes you question your intelligence, and explore dynamic, living environments shaped by their actions. Whether its a retro-inspired pixel RPG or a deep lore-driven quest, we aim to make every project feel like a story worth remembering.

What sets us apart is our dedication to detail. From intricate lore and meaningful quests to original soundtracks. We're not chasing trends—we're telling stories that immerse players on a spiritual scale, and giving a whole new meaning to retro-style pixel RPG's.

We are currently hard at work on expanding our team, and we cant wait to share more with you. In the meantime, check out our other pages and get to know the team behind the very games you pour hours into, or drop us a message—our email is at the bottom of the page!
